Store Policies

Welcome to Dashami Silk. By accessing our website and purchasing our products, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

  • All products are subject to availability.
  • Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason at any time.
  • Colors of products may slightly vary due to lighting and screen settings.

Your privacy is important to us. Dashami Silk collects personal information only to fulfill your orders and improve your shopping experience.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information unless we provide users with advance notice.

We want you to love your purchase. However, please note that we do not offer monetary refunds. We offer replacements only.

  • Replacement requests are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags.
  • We will ship your replacement item within 5-7 business days after receiving and inspecting the original product.
  • Note: Customized or sale items are not eligible for return or replacement.

We ship worldwide. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Domestic Orders: Delivered within 5-7 business days.
International Orders: Delivered within 10-15 business days.

Dashami Silk is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.
